Tokenization

  • Definition: Breaking text into individual units (tokens)
  • Types of tokenization:
    • Word tokenization
    • Sentence tokenization
  • Challenges: Contractions, hyphenated words, URLs
Visual example of tokenization

Text Normalization

  • Converting text to a standard format
  • Techniques:
    • Lowercase conversion
    • Punctuation removal
    • Number handling

Stop Words Removal

  • Definition: Eliminating common, low-information words
  • Benefits: Reduces noise, focuses on meaningful content
  • Caution: Context-dependent, may remove important words in some cases
Word cloud before and after stop word removal

Stemming vs. Lemmatization

  • Stemming: Reducing words to their root form (often crude)
  • Lemmatization: Reducing words to their base form (dictionary form)
  • Comparison:
    • Stemming: Faster, but can produce non-words
    • Lemmatization: More accurate, but slower
